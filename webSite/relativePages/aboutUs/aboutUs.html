ABOUT US

Kookaburra is a company founded in 1890's in Victoria-Australia by two cricketing fans with a love for sport. From decades to decades and generations to generations there was constant rise in their business which was carried further to their sons and grandsons.

For the first 100 years, Kookaburra Sport traded as AG Thompson Pty Ltd, and a number of the Thompson family members are still actively involved in the daily running of the business. Kookaburra Sport employs over 150 employees worldwide as the business continues to grow successfully.

Kookaburra's world best quality attracts every cricketer, due to this reason KOOKABURRA is now in direct relationship with Australian Cricket Board, and it is now sponsoring of all of the sporting equipment to Australian Cricketers.

Kookaburra is now synonymous with Australian sport. Not only is Kookaburra the largest producer of cricket equipment in Australia, Kookaburra cricket bats and ultra-light protective equipment are now also used by the great legendary cricket players.

        IN ASSOCIATION WITH:

Cricket Australia (CA), formerly known as the Australian Cricket Board (ACB), is the governing body for professional and amateur cricket in Australia. It was originally formed in 1905 as the "Australian Board of Control for International Cricket". It is incorporated as an Australian Public Company.

Cricket Australia operates all of the Australian national representative cricket sides, including the Australian cricket team, the Australia national women's cricket team and youth sides as well. CA is also responsible for organising and hosting Test tours and one day internationals with other nations, and scheduling the home international fixtures.

Cricket Australia stands third in the world's richest cricket board list with a net worth of totalling $99 million.
